My Top 10 Favorite Songs

Top 1

Highway 1009

It is written by ENHYPEN for their fans. The number "1009" it represents October 9, which is the official anniversary date when the group's fandom, called ENGENE/S, was announced. The song symbolizes the lifelong journey and connection between ENHYPEN and their fans.

Top 2

Orange Flower (You Complete Me)

It is a feel-good pop track from ENHYPEN that celebrates how love transforms from dark, empty existence into a bright, colorful one. It also conveys the message that finding your soulmate brings warmth, salvation, and ultimate fulfillment to your life.

Top 3

SHOUT OUT

It is a high-energy anthem from ENHYPEN about self-expression, freedom, and breaking free from societal expectations. This song is also called by us fans our Anthem.

Top 4

Fatal Trouble

This song by ENHYPEN refers to a deeply disruptive, ruinous, or destructive problem with irreversible consequences. Us ENGENES call this song (sometimes "a Vampire yearning song")

Top 5

Lucifer

This song by ENHYPEN explores the dark, obsessive feelings of a vampire who loses his loved ones, symbolizing themes of lost light, temptation, and deep emotional anguish.

Top 6

stupid song

This song by Olivia Rodrigo (from her new album you seem pretty sad for a girl so in love) captures the all-consuming, maddening rush of being deeply in love. being in love to the point that cheesy love songs aren't so cheesy after all.

Top 7

my way

This song from olivia Rodrigo is an aggressive, up-tempo anthem about confronting an ex-boyfriend's former partner who refuses to respect boundaries. Like for a girl that can't take a hint.

Top 8

purple

This song by Olivia Rodrigo acts as a metaphor for the toxic nature of enmeshment in a relationship. To the point that everything around you isn't so bad after all.

Top 9

LOUD

This song from NMIXX that expresses the intense frustration of hiding a deep relationship and the overwhelming urge to proclaim it publicly. This song also mean that you can love anyone, and just be yourself.

Top 10

Mono

This song by (G)I-DLE (featuring British rapper Skaiwater) is a celebration of self-acceptance, unity, and stripping away societal noise to focus on fundamental humanity. This song is also about when you love or your being true to yourself, you can Love anyone.
